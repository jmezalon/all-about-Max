About Me

From Uber driver to Software Analyst at Blackstone - my journey into tech has been anything but conventional.

After driving for Uber for over five years, I made a bold decision to pursue my passion for technology. I enrolled in a software engineering bootcamp and immersed myself in web development. The path wasn't easy - I faced setbacks and doubts - but my determination to build technology that improves lives kept me going.

Today, as a Software Analyst at Blackstone, I work extensively with React and Node.js, create data visualizations with Sigma Computing, and explore the frontiers of AI development. I've built applications using ChatGPT, Claude Sonnet 4, and the Claude Code CLI, combining traditional software engineering with cutting-edge AI capabilities.

I believe in building solutions that solve real problems - whether it's helping my community achieve financial goals or making Bible study more accessible. Every project I take on is driven by a desire to create meaningful impact.
