About Me

From Uber driver to Software Analyst at Blackstone - my journey into tech has been anything but conventional.

After driving for Uber for over five years, I made a bold decision to pursue my passion for technology. I enrolled in a software engineering bootcamp and immersed myself in web development. The path wasn't easy - I faced setbacks and doubts - but my determination to build technology that improves lives kept me going.

Today, as a Full-Stack Software Developer at Blackstone (currently an Analyst, on track for Associate promotion), I work extensively with React.js, Express, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. While I'm proficient across the full stack, my true passion lies in frontend development - building beautiful, responsive, modern websites with React.

I've embraced AI as a powerful development tool, primarily building projects with Anthropic's Claude Sonnet, and also working with OpenAI's ChatGPT. I believe in combining traditional software engineering principles with cutting-edge AI capabilities to build solutions that solve real problems.

Whether it's helping my Caribbean community achieve financial goals through collaborative savings, or making Bible study more accessible through technology - every project I take on is driven by a desire to create meaningful impact.
